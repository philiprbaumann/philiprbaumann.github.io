I really wanted to love this series—and I did, at the start. The worldbuilding and Bridge 4 pulled me all the way in. But by the end, I was ready for it to be over. I’ll always be Bridge 4, but if I have to read Kaladin therapizing someone one more time, I might need therapy myself.
